Privacy Policy

Last updated · July 2026

Next is a local-first calendar and to-do app for Mac and iPhone, built to keep your data on your devices.

No accounts · No tracking · No servers

Data we collect

None. Next has no servers, no account system, and no analytics or tracking. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information.

Your calendar and reminders

With your permission, Next reads and writes events and reminders in the built-in Calendar and Reminders on your Mac or iPhone via Apple's EventKit framework. This data stays on your device and within the Apple, iCloud, Google, or Outlook accounts you've configured in System Settings (macOS) or Settings (iOS). Next never sends it to us or to any third party.

Location search

When you type a location for an event, Next sends that text to Apple Maps (MapKit) to fetch address suggestions. This request is handled by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy, and it is the app's only network request.

Third parties

We share no data with third parties, because we collect none.

Children

Next collects no data from anyone, including children.
